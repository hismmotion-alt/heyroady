The Classic California Coast Drive
Few drives rival the stretch of Pacific Coast Highway between Los Angeles and San Diego. You'll pass through legendary surf towns, palm-lined boardwalks, and sun-bleached cliffs before arriving in one of California's most beloved cities.
🏖 Beach 🌮 Food 🌊 Scenic 📸 Photo spots
Start in Manhattan Beach
Begin your trip at Manhattan Beach's famous Strand — a paved path stretching the length of the beach. Grab coffee, watch the surfers, and soak in the classic LA beach vibe before hitting the road.
💡 Local tip: Parking on the Strand fills up by 9am on weekends. Arrive early or park a few blocks inland.
Did you know?
The stretch of PCH between LA and San Diego passes through 7 distinct beach cities, each with its own personality — from the surfer culture of Huntington to the artsy vibe of Laguna Beach.
Don't Miss: Laguna Beach
Laguna Beach is arguably the most scenic stop on this route. Dramatic cliffs, hidden coves, and a thriving arts scene make it worth at least 2 hours. Walk down to Victoria Beach for the famous Pirate Tower.
📸 Photo spot: Heisler Park overlook — best view of the cove at golden hour.
