LA → San Diego
The Classic California Coast Drive
One of the most iconic road trips in America, this route hugs the Pacific Coast Highway from Los Angeles down to San Diego — passing through surf towns, beach boardwalks, and seaside cliffs.
Stop 1 — Manhattan Beach
Start here for the classic LA beach vibe. Grab breakfast at one of the cafes lining the Strand before heading south.
Stop 2 — Huntington Beach
Surf City USA is worth a 1-2 hour stop. Walk the pier, grab fish tacos, watch the surfers.
